My journey into UX

When I was younger, I always loved playing with computers. I would create song lyric videos and post them onto Youtube so others can sing along. I would also edit videos and post them as well. One day, I was watching a Youtube and I came across this youtuber that goes by the name "nameishana." She would film her daily vlogs about how she got into UX and show her desktop setup and everything. I was immediately intrigued. I instatnly knew that is what I wanted to pursue. I wanted to be a User experience designer. So I researched different programs and bootcamps, and I came across Humber's MMDD program. I applied and got in. I'm always learning new things and can't wait to start designing with a great team!
